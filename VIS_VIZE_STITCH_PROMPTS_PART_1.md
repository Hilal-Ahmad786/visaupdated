# VİS VİZE — Stitch UI/UX Prompts, Part 1

**Brand:** VİS VİZE RANDEVU HİZMETLERİ  
**Scope:** 20 public-facing website screens  
**Primary goal:** Google Ads and organic traffic conversion through a prominent phone number and form-first user journey.  
**Design reference:** Use the uploaded VİS VİZE logo. Follow the conversion logic of strong visa lead-generation sites, but do not copy their visual design.

---

## Shared Design System for All 20 Screens

Use these rules consistently on every screen unless the individual prompt explicitly overrides them.

### Brand and Visual Direction

- Brand Navy: `#0C2448`
- Deep Navy: `#071A35`
- Brand Gold: `#B88522`
- Gold Hover: `#9D7018`
- Soft Champagne: `#E0C99A`
- Warm Gold Surface: `#F7F0E2`
- Page Background: `#F7F9FC`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary Text: `#10213D`
- Secondary Text: `#59677A`
- Border: `#D9E0EA`
- Success: `#16845B`
- Warning: `#C77912`
- Error: `#C63D45`

Use **Manrope** for headings, labels, navigation, buttons, phone numbers, and important UI text. Use **Inter** for body copy, helper text, legal notices, and long-form content.

The design must feel premium, trustworthy, human, modern, calm, conversion-focused, and internationally relevant. Do not make it look like a government portal, airline booking site, cheap template, or competitor clone.

### Conversion Rules

Primary conversion actions:

1. **Ücretsiz Ön Başvuru**
2. **Hemen Ara**

Secondary action:

- **WhatsApp’tan Yaz**

The phone number and form must be highly visible. On mobile, use a fixed bottom bar on important public pages:

- Left: **Hemen Ara**
- Right: context-aware action such as **Ön Başvuru**, **Devam Et**, **Gönder**, or **Randevu Talebi**

WhatsApp must remain secondary, must not open automatically, and must not cover form controls.

### Legal and Trust Rules

Never claim:

- Guaranteed visa approval
- Guaranteed appointment
- Guaranteed processing duration
- Guaranteed document acceptance
- Unsupported success rates
- Official government, embassy, consulate, or application-center affiliation

Use a visible statement where relevant:

> VİS VİZE resmi bir konsolosluk, büyükelçilik, vize başvuru merkezi veya devlet kurumu değildir. Vize başvurularına yönelik danışmanlık ve destek hizmeti sunar. Nihai kararlar ilgili resmi makamlar tarafından verilir.

### Standard Header

Use a two-level sticky header:

- Deep-navy utility bar with service area, working hours, and phone number
- White main header with uploaded logo, navigation, phone block, and gold CTA

Standard navigation:

- Ana Sayfa
- Vize Ülkeleri
- Hizmetler
- Vize Süreci
- Blog
- S.S.S.
- Hakkımızda
- İletişim

### Standard Footer

Use a deep-navy footer with:

- Logo and short brand explanation
- Phone, WhatsApp, email, address, working hours
- Relevant page links
- Legal links
- Copyright
- Private-consultancy disclaimer

All phone numbers, email, address, working hours, page content, legal copy, forms, CTAs, and tracking configuration must be editable from the admin panel.

# Shared Final Quality Checklist

Apply this checklist to every screen:

## Conversion

- Phone number is easy to find
- Primary form or relevant CTA is visible early
- Mobile fixed action is context-aware
- WhatsApp remains secondary
- Bottom click-to-call section exists where appropriate

## Trust

- No guarantees
- No fake statistics
- No fake reviews
- No fake credentials
- No government-style implication
- Private-consultancy notice is readable

## Forms

- Visible labels
- Clear required fields
- Inline validation
- Error summary where needed
- Loading, success, failure, duplicate states
- KVKK consent
- Optional marketing consent not preselected
- Correct mobile keyboard
- Accessible controls

## Accessibility

- One clear H1
- Logical heading hierarchy
- Strong contrast
- Minimum 44px targets
- Visible focus states
- Keyboard accessibility
- Screen-reader labels
- No information conveyed only through color, icon, or flag
- Reduced-motion support

## Performance

- No heavy video backgrounds
- Optimized images
- Lazy loading below fold
- Lightweight SVG icons
- Minimal layout shift
- No auto-opening chat
- No excessive animation
- Server-rendered content where appropriate

## Admin and CMS

All content, forms, countries, services, guides, FAQs, navigation, footer, contact details, legal copy, tracking IDs, SEO metadata, and visibility states must be editable without modifying source code.
